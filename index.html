About me

[ Illustration / Photo ]

I'm a Graphic Designer (UNNE), but my work lives on the screen. I specialize in UI Development and visual design, merging my background in visual communication and digital illustration with technical implementation using HTML, CSS, and Tailwind.

Through my freelance experience, I've learned how to turn real-world needs into cohesive visual identities. My main passion is crafting interfaces directly with code, ensuring that a design never loses its essence when translated to the browser. That's why I design with development in mind—balancing aesthetics with accessibility and pixel-perfect layouts.

For over a decade, I've been dedicated to strength training and fitness. The discipline, consistency, and resilience I've built in the gym are the exact same tools I bring to my work today. I'm looking to join a team where I can use my visual eye and technical skills to bridge the gap between great design and flawless execution.
